Privacy Policy for Dhikr - Quran Tracker

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

In Short: Dhikr app does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All your data stays on your device only.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Dhikr - Quran Tracker ("we," "our," or "the app"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect

Dhikr app is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We do NOT collect, access, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Any other personal or sensitive data

3. Data Storage

All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Quran Reading Progress: Your Juz and Ruku tracking information
  • Dhikr Counts: Your daily and total Dhikr counter data
  • Custom Dhikr: Any custom Dhikr you create
  • App Preferences: Theme settings (light/dark mode) and language preferences

This data is stored in your device's local storage using SQLite database and SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

4. Permissions

The app requires minimal permissions:

  • Storage: Used only to save your progress, preferences, and Dhikr counts locally on your device
  • Notifications (Optional): Used only if you choose to enable reminder notifications for Dhikr or Quran reading

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not enable any data collection or transmission.

5. Third-Party Services

Dhikr app does NOT integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage or backup services

Your data stays completely private on your device.

6. Internet Connection

The app does NOT require an internet connection to function. All features work completely offline. The app does not transmit any data over the internet.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of children's data being collected, stored, or shared. The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device secured with a password, PIN, or biometric lock
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date
  • Using device backup features if you want to preserve your app data

9. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all app data from your device
  • Clearing app data in your device settings will reset the app and delete all stored information
  • Individual Dhikr history can be managed within the app

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app and on this page. The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information for us to access, modify, or delete. You have complete ownership and control of all data stored in the app on your device.

12. Compliance

This app complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Google Play Store policies
  • Apple App Store guidelines

13. Open Source Libraries

The app uses the following open-source libraries, which do not collect user data:

  • Flutter SDK: UI framework (no data collection)
  • SQLite: Local database (stores data only on device)
  • SharedPreferences: Local preference storage (device only)
  • Provider: State management (no external communication)
